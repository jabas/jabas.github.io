About Me

As the daughter of an aerobic instructor and nutrition expert, I always knew I would be involved in Health and Fitness. With a degree in musical theatre and dance, I spent much of my time leading cast workouts and helping others order off menus! Once I became a mom, I knew it was time to take my passion to the next level. Today, I help others understand how their healthy way of life fits in with their current lifestyle.

“Be sure you put your feet in the right place, then stand firm.”

— Abraham Lincoln
