Content Sections

▼

.content-section surrounds a logical group of content to provide additional padding above and below.

This is the most appropriate element to put background colors onto.

The .content-mt class adds the equivalent measure of space that .content-section applies, but as margin on top of element. This is usually used when the preceeding content is not within a .content-section.

Content Inverse

▼

The .content-inverse class is added to sections where text needs to be reversed (either due to background color or image.)

It alters the following elements:

All Header Tags (including .section-header)

Text content including paragraphs, lists, muted text, anchors, and underlined anchors.

Primary Outline Secondary Outline
