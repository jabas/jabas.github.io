About Me

I took my first group fitness class in 1982 and was immediately hooked! I began teaching classes that same year. 34 years later, I love it more now than ever before. As I Pilates and yoga instructor I feel blessed and inspired every day by the beautiful clients I get to work with. I get a front row seat as I watch them struggle and ultimately succeed. 12 of my clients have been with me for over 5 years. 5 have been with me for a decade.

About Me

As the daughter of an aerobic instructor and nutrition expert, I always knew I would be involved in Health and Fitness. With a degree in musical theatre and dance, I spent much of my time leading cast workouts and helping others order off menus! Once I became a mom, I knew it was time to take my passion to the next level. Today, I help others understand how their healthy way of life fits in with their current lifestyle.
