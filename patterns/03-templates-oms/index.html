Student Membership Details

Discounted monthly rate for individuals at least 12 years of age that are an actively enrolled secondary or post-secondary student. Students receive access to one club from May 1 to August 31. This membership will then automatically convert September 1 to non-access “Student Flex” status at the monthly club flex rate of $10 to $20, depending on club, and will provide intermittent access during fall, winter, and spring break periods from September 1 to April 30.

26 and Under Membership Details

Discounted monthly rate for access to one club. Individual must be at least 12 years but less than 27 years of age. When member reaches age 27, this membership will automatically be converted to the then-prevailing individual membership rate at the applicable club membership level.

Junior Executive Membership Details

Discounted monthly rate for individuals at least 12 years but less than 30 years of age. When member reaches age 30, this membership will automatically be converted to the then-prevailing individual membership rate at the applicable club membership level.

Membership dues, levels, fees, benefits, services and amenities vary by club and are subject to change at any time. Other terms and conditions apply. *Show additional Monthly, 26 and Under, Student, and Day Pass terms and conditions.

Monthly memberships are month-to-month access memberships that continue until canceled upon 30 days’ advance written notice.
