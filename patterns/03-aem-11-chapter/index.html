Chapter Break

A chapter break can be put inside of chapter to create an equally spaced block without closing the chapter. This can either be with or without a full-length horizontal rule. Like the chapter, it can also include a centered heading and intro block.

Chapter Inset

A chapter inset is a special version of a chapter where the content is contained either in a border or shaded background, but the edges are constrained to the page container. These have additional margin at the bottom to separate them from the next chapter.
